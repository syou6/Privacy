Privacy Policy for FocusTodo

Last updated: July 11, 2025

Introduction

FocusTodo ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Authentication credentials (encrypted)

Task Data

We store the tasks you create, including:

  • Task descriptions
  • Task completion status
  • Creation and modification timestamps

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the FocusTodo service
  • Authenticate your account
  • Sync your tasks across devices
  • Send important service-related notifications

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure with:

  • Industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Use your data for advertising

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your task data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

Children's Privacy

FocusTodo is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
